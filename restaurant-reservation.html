Restaurant Reservation Eligibility
Assingment 1: Restaurant Reservation Eligibility

You are working on a restaurant reservation system, and you need to determine if a customer is eligible for a reservation based on their preferences and availability. Write a JavaScript program that prompts the user for their preferred time slot (morning or evening) and the number of available seats. The program should check the eligibility of the customer by applying the following conditions:

  1. If the preferred time slot is "morning" and there are at least 3 available seats, the reservation is eligible.
  2. If the preferred time slot is "evening" and there are at least 5 available seats, the reservation is eligible.
  3. Otherwise, the reservation is not eligible.
